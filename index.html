About

My name is Amstel-Dev, and I'm a passionate Computer Science student with a diverse range of skills and experiences.

With a focus on Web Development and AI development using Python, I have gained practical knowledge in these areas and love exploring the endless possibilities they offer. I enjoy creating dynamic and user-friendly websites, as well as working on innovative AI projects that push the boundaries of technology.

In addition to my programming expertise, I also have a knack for Computer and Smartphone Repairs. Whether it's troubleshooting hardware issues or optimizing software performance, I love tackling challenges head-on to ensure devices are running smoothly and efficiently.

I have also dabbled in the world of Graphic Designing and Video Editing. I find immense joy in combining creativity with technology to craft visually captivating designs and engaging multimedia content.

My adventures in the Linux realm have further expanded my skill set. I am proficient in using Linux systems such as Debian and Arch linux distribution. I have even taken on the exciting endeavor of building Linux from scratch, an experience that has deepened my understanding of operating systems and their inner workings.

As a lifelong learner, I am always seeking new opportunities to grow and refine my skills. I approach each project with dedication and a commitment to delivering high-quality results.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope we can collaborate on exciting projects or connect over shared interests in the world of technology.
